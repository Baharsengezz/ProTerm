How To Play?

Hangman is a simple word guessing game. Players try to figure out an unknown word by guessing and using informations of programming terminology about letters. If too many letters which do not appear in the word are guessed, the player is hanged and loses.

The player starts with a 4-letter word first. As you level, the word length increases. It loses 10 points for every letter it knows wrong. In this case, the tree parts are which are hanged the tree of the game simulation, and in the order: head, body, left leg, right leg, left arm, right arm.
